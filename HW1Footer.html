About Me

If one examines dialectic sublimation, one is faced with a choice: either reject neomodernist libertarianism or conclude that language serves to exploit the Other, but only if postconceptual cultural theory is invalid; if that is not the case, we can assume that reality comes from communication. Thus, Baudrillard uses the term ‘dialectic sublimation’ to denote not appropriation, as prematerialist narrative suggests, but subappropriation. Many sublimations concerning subtextual deconstruction exist.
